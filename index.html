🔒 Privacy Policy

Who we are

Micro Macro Nutri Labs operates the Micro Macro Tracker mobile application ("the App"). This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Data we collect

  • Account information – your email address and display name when you register or sign in with Google or Apple.
  • Food & nutrition data – meals and nutrients you log within the App, stored locally on your device.
  • Authentication tokens – short-lived tokens managed by Firebase Authentication to keep you signed in securely.

Data we do NOT collect

  • We do not collect precise location data.
  • We do not collect payment information directly (handled by Apple / Google).
  • We do not track your activity across other apps or websites.

How we use your data

  • To authenticate you and secure your account.
  • To store and display your personal nutrition log within the App.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-party services

The App uses the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase Authentication (Google LLC) – account management and secure sign-in.
  • Google Sign-In – optional OAuth sign-in via your Google account.
  • Sign in with Apple – optional OAuth sign-in via your Apple ID.

Data retention & deletion

Locally stored nutrition data remains on your device until you uninstall the App or clear its data. To delete your account and associated authentication data, use the "Delete account" option in the App settings or contact us at the address below.

Children's privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The effective date below will be updated accordingly. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance.
