The hidden story in your food

Antinutrients block the minerals you're trying so hard to get.

Most nutrition apps show you the nutrients in your food. But they don't tell you how much of that iron, zinc, or calcium your body can actually absorb.

Micro Macro Tracker tracks antinutrients — the compounds that bind to minerals and reduce absorption — so you get the full picture of what your diet is actually delivering to your body.

  • 🌾
    Phytic Acid
    Found in grains, legumes, nuts. Binds iron, zinc, calcium. Reduced by soaking, sprouting, fermenting.
  • 🥬
    Oxalates
    Found in spinach, almonds, sweet potato. Binds calcium and iron. Cooking reduces levels.
  • 🫘
    Lectins
    Found in raw beans, lentils, nightshades. Can interfere with nutrient absorption. Destroyed by heat.
  • 🍵
    Tannins
    Found in tea, coffee, red wine. Inhibit iron absorption. Avoid drinking with iron-rich meals.
  • 🥦
    Goitrogens
    Found in cruciferous vegetables. Can interfere with thyroid iodine uptake in large amounts.
